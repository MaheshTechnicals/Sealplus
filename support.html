Seal Plus is built and maintained independently. Your support keeps the project fast, secure, and regularly updated for everyone.

1 Why support is needed

Seal Plus is free and open source. Supporting the project helps cover the real costs of building a reliable downloader and keeping it updated as platforms change frequently.

  • Development time for new features and bug fixes
  • Testing across devices and Android versions
  • Domain, hosting, and release maintenance
  • Design, translations, and accessibility improvements

2 How your support helps

Every contribution directly improves the app. It keeps updates flowing and helps ensure the project stays independent and community focused.

  • Faster updates when websites change
  • Stable releases with better QA
  • Long-term maintenance and security updates
