Seal Plus Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction

Seal Plus respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information when you use Seal Plus.

Information Collection

Seal Plus does not collect personal information such as name, email, location, or identity data.

  • No account registration required
  • No personal data collection
  • No tracking of user activity

How Seal Plus Works

Seal Plus processes media links locally on your device. Downloads and processing activities occur directly on your device without transferring personal data to external servers controlled by the developer.

Permissions Usage

  • Storage Permission — Save downloaded media files
  • Network Access — Download requested content
  • Notification Permission — Show download progress

Third-Party Services

Seal Plus may interact with external websites only when users provide links manually. The developer does not control third-party platform privacy policies.

Data Security

All downloads and processing occur locally on the user's device. Seal Plus does not store user information on remote servers.

Children's Privacy

Seal Plus does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page.
